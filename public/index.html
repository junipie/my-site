Montana Code School is a state-wide initiative dedicated to training the state’s next generation of junior developers with 2016 classes being held in Missoula and Bozeman.

We offer intensive, fully-immersive coding boot-camp programs designed to produce highly employable software developers ready to hit the ground running in Montana’s thriving community of startups and tech businesses.
