Oh Hey There.

I'm Juniper. I love to make great products, and I serve my fellow humans effectively and passionately.

I recently made a 180º shift in my career, and I'm studying to be a developer at Montana Code School. My general interests are the same -- I like projects, great teams, and solving the unsolvable -- but now there's code involved! As I graduate from my program in late December, I'm on the hunt for a development job.

I still haven’t decided what I want to do “when I grow up” – but I love people, and I love the satisfaction of a job well-done. I want to work with great people, doing something that adds value to the world, for a company I respect and believe in.
